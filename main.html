Categories

This page shows all the past years photo contests categories plus whats to come this year for July and August! Each of the 3 months the contest is held there are 3 categories per month. The topic can be on anything that is appropriate. Some of the topics can include vehicles, sports, weather, food, animals and much more. Every year we have had awesome photos taken on the topics picked and this year hopefully all participants can continue that trend. Remember that participants can also recommend future contest topics by simply listing any ideas in the live chat section and we will consider them.
